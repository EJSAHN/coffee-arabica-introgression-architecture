# Analysis notes

- Only explicitly labelled sgC or sgE pseudomolecules are retained in the corresponding subgenome analysis.
- Arabica-only PCA is used for within-Arabica inference because inclusion of the five diploid references alters detailed ordination geometry. Full-panel PCA is retained for progenitor context.
- Chromosome 4 is identified through a genome-wide scan rather than a targeted chromosome-only analysis.
- Operational interval endpoints are used for summary and sensitivity analysis; exact base-pair boundaries are not treated as resolved.
- Direct sequence alignment supports chromosome-level correspondence to ET-39 chromosome 4 pseudomolecules, while incomplete coverage and local orientation differences preclude a single exact liftover interval.
- Candidate annotations are restricted to direct alignment-supported target blocks and remain provisional.
